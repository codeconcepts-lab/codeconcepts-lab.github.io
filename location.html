Directions

For better directions please read the below information:

You can type in “Battlefields Pub and Grub” into Google Maps.

This will take you to a restaurant on the R103 in between Mooi River and Estcourt.

This restaurant is directly opposite the dirt road you need to take to get to Bush Creek Guest House.

  • If you are coming from Estcourt / Midway N3 you will turn left onto the Dirt Road.
  • If you are coming from Mooi River / Durban area you will turn right onto the Dirt Road.

There will be signs.

You need to drive 5,7kms on this dirt road. Keep on this dirt road do not via off.

Please keep following the Bush Creek Signs all the way to Bush Creek Guest House Black electric gate.
