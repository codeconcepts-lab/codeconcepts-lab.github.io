Local Activities & Attractions

Explore the Neighbourhood

Venture beyond the farm and discover the attractions and activities available in the surrounding Midlands area.

Weenen Nature Reserve

The reserve offers extensive game-viewing facilities, guided walks, environmental education, and three picnic sites which makes it ideal for a day visit.

(036) 354-7013 Directions

Piggly Wiggly Country Village

It features a variety of family run-eateries and artisanal shops. You can explore unique local stores offering hand crafted goods, enjoy gourmet treats and experience a range of activities for both kids and adults.

(082) 511-2769 Directions

Nelson Mandela Capture Site

Nelson Mandela was arrested on 05 August 1962 outside Howick, KZN. The Capture site commemorates this moment in history with a Museum and a world renowned Sculpture. Coffee and Gifts shops on site.

(072) 351-0967 Directions

Golf At Mooi River Country Club

This scenic course is nestled amongst farmlands and mountain ranges. There is a bar, restaurant and kids play ground. The Mooi River Golf Course has 11 holes with 18 tee boxes and a par 72, with a 71 rating.

(033) 940-2041 Directions

Wagon Drift Dam And Reserve

Wagendrift Dam and Moor Park Nature Reserve is situated just off the N3 highway near Estcourt. The reserve is roughly 980 ha in extent and includes a popular picnic and fishing spot.

(072) 800-3893 Directions

Nottingham Road Park Run

It is a 5km run or walk starting at 8am. The event takes places at the Windmills Ultrastop, N3 Nottingham Road, Curries Post Interchange, 3280. Experience a beautiful run/walk in the Blue Crane Nature Reserve.

(033) 266-7021 Directions

Epic Carting, Midlands

This amazing venue has an outdoor go-kart, target Shooting, and Sandart. There is also a Lifestyle Gifting shop and a Coffee Cafe (serving snacks, light lunches, milkshakes and cappuccinos).

(079) 320-1770 Directions
